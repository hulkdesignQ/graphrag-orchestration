Why Today's Document AI Falls Short

ChatGPT and Claude can upload files — but uploading isn't understanding. They still guess. Evidoc knows.

ChatGPT / Claude / Gemini

Upload files and get answers — but no persistent memory, no real proof, and no cross-document intelligence.

  • ✕ Re-upload every session: No persistent knowledge base. Context is lost when the chat ends.
  • ✕ Generates plausible text: Answers are synthesized by the LLM, not retrieved. Hallucinations look identical to facts.
  • ✕ Vague citations: "Based on the document…" with no way to click, verify, or trace back to the exact source.
  • ✕ Expensive at scale: Every query re-processes all your documents. Costs add up fast with large file sets.

Evidoc

A persistent, private knowledge base that builds a Knowledge Graph across all your documents — every answer is traceable.

  • ✓ Upload once, query forever: Documents are indexed into a persistent Knowledge Graph. Always available, always up to date.
  • ✓ Retrieves, never fabricates: Answers are extracted via deterministic graph traversal — not generated by an LLM.
  • ✓ Click-to-verify citations: Every citation links to the exact sentence, highlighted on the original page. One click to confirm.
  • ✓ Index once, query cheaply: Your documents are pre-processed into a Knowledge Graph. Each query is a fast graph lookup, not a full re-read.
