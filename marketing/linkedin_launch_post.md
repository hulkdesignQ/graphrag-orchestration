# Evidoc — LinkedIn Launch Post

Last year, a property management company told us they spent an entire Friday cross-checking a contractor invoice against the service agreement.

Page 12 said one rate. Page 37 said another. They caught it — but only because someone happened to remember.

Here's the uncomfortable truth about document work: the mistakes you catch are luck. The ones you miss are liability.

So we built **Evidoc**.

Upload your documents. Ask a question. Get an answer with every claim linked to the **exact sentence** in the original document — highlighted, clickable, verifiable.

Not "it's probably on page 12."
The actual sentence. The actual words.

**How it works:**
1. Upload your files — PDFs, Word docs, spreadsheets, presentations, images, and more
2. Evidoc connects information across all your documents automatically
3. Ask anything in your language — every answer traces back to the source, one click to verify

**What makes this different from ChatGPT, Claude, and Gemini:**
- They generate answers you have to hope are right. Evidoc pulls answers **directly from your documents** — if it's not in your files, it won't make it up.
- Every citation is clickable — see the exact text highlighted on the original page.
- It connects the dots across documents — "Acme Corp" on page 3 and "ACME Corporation" on page 47? Same entity, linked automatically.
- Works in 13 languages with built-in translation and voice input.
